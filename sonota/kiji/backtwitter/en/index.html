Install
Step.1 - Download
Please download from "Download" at the below of this site.(safety)
Step.2 - [.zip]Decompression
Unzip the downloaded zip file using your usual method.*Please do not unzip if you are using Firefox.
Step.3 - Install(Chrome)
Open Chrome, select "Manage Extensions" from the puzzle mark in the upper right corner, turn on "Developer Mode" in the upper right corner of the page, press "Load Unpackaged Extensions" on the left side of the page, and select the folder you just unzipped.

The installation is now complete!
Step.3 - Install(Firefox)
Open Firefox, type "about:debugging#/runtime/this-firefox" in the address bar to access it, press "Load temporary add-ons" in the page, select the zip file you just downloaded, then once you press " Open "ğ•", press the "Extensions" (puzzle piece button) in the upper right corner, press the "gear button" to the right of BackTwitter in the frame, and check the "on twitter site" box.

The installation is now complete!
Step.3 - Install(Edge)
Open Edge, select "Manage Extensions" from the puzzle mark in the upper right corner, turn on "Developer Mode" on the left side of the page, press "Extract and Load" on the right side of "Installed Extensions", and select the folder you just extracted.

The installation is now complete!
Step.3 - Install(Vivaldi)
Open Vivaldi, click the logo in the upper left corner, go to "Tools", then "Extensions", turn on "Developer Mode" in the upper right corner of the page, click "Load Unpackaged Extensions" on the left side of the page, and select the folder you have just unzipped.

The installation is now complete!
Step.3 - Install(OperaGX)
Open OperaGX, click "Extensions" (box button) on the left toolbar, turn on "Developer Mode" in the upper right corner of the page, click "Load Unpackaged Extensions" on the left side of the page, and select the folder you just unzipped.

The installation is now complete!
